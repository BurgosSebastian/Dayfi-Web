Buy now Pay later (Q4)

Lower the barrier to purchasing NFTs without hefty upfront costs.

Dayfi trailblazing protocol allows NFTs to be more affordable to everyone.

How does NFT pay later work?

  • Once you have selected the NFT you want to buy you will be asked to pay a down payment.
  • When you have paid the down payment the NFT is transferred to a secure vault meanwhile utilize the 100% utility of the NFT.
  • The remaining amount shall be deducted in a series of EMIs for the next 3 Months.
  • When all the instalments have been paid, the NFT will get transferred from the secure vault to your wallet.

Why Use Dayfi BNPL(Buy now pay later)

  1. Immediate access to all benefits of the NFT, such as play-to-earn, community events, membership rights, token airdrops, and whitelist inclusion
  2. Lower the barrier to access NFTs
  3. Enjoy all benefits of the NFT you want now. Pay the full price later.
  4. Explore metaverse without paying the hefty upfront cost.
