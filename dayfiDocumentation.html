Introducing Dayfi

At Dayfi, We are introducing default “ Financial utilities ’’ for NFTs to have the ability to be loaned, rented & Bought affordably, This brings confidence for an investor to invest in digital assets & later capitalise on unique utilities. Thus making the overall Asset class more stable & liquid.


Imagine renting out NFT tickets or an In game exclusive limited wearables/digital fashion NFTs asset . What about loaning your Blue-chip NFT & generate passive income while it is lying dead in your wallet? if this does not make sense then what’s better than being a “Digital landlord” Oh yeah! pretty fascinating leasing out digital lands? sounds $$$.


Founded in 2022 . Dayfi's goal is to create open, safe, and strong financial products for NFTs that are accessible to everyone, anywhere.


Dayfi is built on top of major Blockchains that allow essential financial services for NFTs such as renting, lending, and Buy now pay later.


Decentralized protocols are non-custodial, meaning the services never rely on the custody of any middleman or intermediary.



Our vision


We aspire to create a peer-to-peer decentralized infrastructure that allows trust-less short-term digital asset rentals and leverages decentralized lending to provide solutions against mispricing and market volatility.


We aim to minimize dependency on liquidity providers & increase peer-peer trade transparency.



How does Dayfi´s Protocol Work


Dayfi allows owners of NFT assets to rent out their assets for a defined price, and duration. Allowing users to utilize 100% utility during the transaction period while restricting key actions like ( Transfer, share or burn ), Vault allows them to deep dive into the ecosystem via “ wallet connect” .


Easy Integration via its plug & play ( API SDKs ) powers NFT Marketplaces, Metaverse Projects, Decentralised Finance, and Token-based projects. It allows Dayfi to add instant liquidity to NFTs via Collateral less Renting and make them more affordable & liquid throughout the spectrum Of ANTI — BNPL. Where we remove dependency on liquidity partners to finance the trade and instead leverage the P2P consensus to enable the trade.
